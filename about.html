ABOUT US

Ast began in 2020 with no plan, no rules, and zero corporate ties. Just raw instinct, urban energy, and the chaos of everyday life. What started as a simple idea quickly evolved into a movement driven by creativity, authenticity, and fearless self-expression.

Inspired by street culture and real stories, Ast creates pieces that reflect individuality and confidence. Every design represents a mindset bold, unapologetic, and always evolving with the rhythm of the city.
